Flying Dog Brewery

If you’re over 21 and like the taste of beer, head over to Flying Dog Brewery, located at 4607 Wedgewood Boulevard in Frederick, Maryland. The tasting room is open Thursday and Friday 3-8 pm, Saturday 12-8 pm and Sundays 12-6 pm. They have a large patio with a food truck. You’ll need to show ID to enter (no fakes allowed). Free tours of the brewery are offered Thursday through Sunday afternoons with a reservation, which can be made here.
